### Figure Caption: Marginal Cost of Additional Support

This figure reports the incremental cost of improving escape-from-C performance under larger total-step budgets.
For each adjacent MAX_STEPS interval, marginal cost is defined as the additional mean steps used divided by the additional escape-from-C rate (%).

Lower values indicate more efficient support.
Higher values indicate diminishing efficiency, meaning that additional steps are required to produce the same improvement in student outcomes.
