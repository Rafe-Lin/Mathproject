### Figure Caption: Strategy Comparison under Fixed Budgets (Weak Students)

This figure compares Baseline, Rule-Based, and Adaptive (Ours) under identical MAX_STEPS budgets for Weak (C) students, with escape-from-C defined as final mastery >= 0.60.
Across fixed budgets, the strongest strategy differs by budget, so system-level effectiveness should be interpreted condition-wise.

- MAX_STEPS=50: Adaptive difference is -7.50 pp vs Baseline and -7.90 pp vs Rule-Based.
- MAX_STEPS=70: Adaptive difference is -6.80 pp vs Baseline and -3.20 pp vs Rule-Based.
- MAX_STEPS=90: Adaptive difference is -9.00 pp vs Baseline and -3.90 pp vs Rule-Based.
