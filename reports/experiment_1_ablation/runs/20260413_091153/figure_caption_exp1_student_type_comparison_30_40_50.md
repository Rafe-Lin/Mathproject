### Figure Caption: Experiment 1 Student-Level Strategy Comparison (MAX_STEPS = 30/40/50)

This figure compares success rate across three strategies (Baseline, Rule-Based, Adaptive) for three student levels under three step budgets (30, 40, 50).

Adaptive (Ours) shows the strongest relative performance across conditions, with clear gains in the mid-level and higher-performing groups as budget increases.

The largest strategy gap appears at MAX_STEPS=40 for Average (B) (Adaptive lead: 68.0%).

The most difficult setting is Weak (C) at MAX_STEPS=30, where all strategies remain comparatively low.
