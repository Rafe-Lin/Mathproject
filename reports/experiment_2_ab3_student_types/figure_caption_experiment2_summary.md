### Figure Caption: Experiment 2 Policy Behavior Summary
This figure compares policy allocation across Careless, Average, and Weak groups under AB3.
Both bars are computed from real step-level logs: remediation ratio = remediation steps / active steps,
mainline ratio = mainline steps / active steps.
Key finding: AB3 allocates different remediation intensity by student type, with weaker learners receiving
a larger remediation share and stronger learners remaining more frequently on mainline.
